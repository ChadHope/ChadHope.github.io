1. How to Check Airpods Battery Percentage on Android#

AirPods are a great accessory to make life easier, but it’s easy to forget to charge them. Fortunately, Android devices now have the ability to check how much battery power is left in your AirPods. Checking your AirPods battery percentage on Android is quick and simple. Here’s how you can do it:

First, make sure your AirPods are paired with your Android device. On the device, open the quick settings and tap on the Bluetooth icon. Scroll down and select the AirPods. This will open the AirPods quick settings page. Here, you will be able to check the battery percentage of each AirPod earbud.

If you are using an older version of Android, the battery percentage will not be available in the quick settings page. Instead, you can go to Settings - Connected Devices - Bluetooth and select your AirPods. If they are connected to the device, you can then check the battery percentage.

The battery percentage will also be visible when you access the AirPods in your media player. If you’re using, for example, Google Play Music, you can check the percentage by tapping on the AirPods icon in the bottom of the screen.

Lastly, you can also check the battery percentage of your AirPods in the Apple Music app on Android. All you need to do is tap on the headphone icon located at the bottom of the app and the battery percentage will be displayed.

By following these simple steps you can easily check the battery percentage of your AirPods on any Android device.
