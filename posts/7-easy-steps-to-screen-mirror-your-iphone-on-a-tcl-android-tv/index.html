Q2 : Is screen mirroring the same as casting? A2 : No, they are not the same. Screen mirroring is the process of mirroring the contents of your device’s screen to a TV. This is done by connecting your device to a TV via an HDMI cable or through wireless means. Casting, on the other hand, is when you stream content from another device to your TV. This is done by connecting your device to the same WiFi network as your TV and using an app or service to stream the content.
