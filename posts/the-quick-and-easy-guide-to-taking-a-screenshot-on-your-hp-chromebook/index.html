1. Overview of Screenshot Options on HP Chromebook#

The simplest way to capture a screenshot on your HP Chromebook is to press the ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Window Switcher’ keys at the same time. This will save a picture of the entire screen to your Chromebook’s ‘Downloads’ folder.

For more control over the screenshot, you can use the ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Shift’ buttons alongside the ‘Window Switcher’ key. This will bring up a menu of options for area, window, and full-screen screenshots. From here you can choose to save the image to any folder on your Chromebook.

You can also capture a screenshot using the stylus on your HP Chromebook. Press the ‘Pen’ button, and you will see a number of pen options. From here, press the ‘Capture Region’ button to draw a box around the area you would like to capture.
