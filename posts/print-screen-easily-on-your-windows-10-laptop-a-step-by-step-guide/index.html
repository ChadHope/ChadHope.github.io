1. Steps to take a Print Screen on Windows 10 Laptop#

Taking a print screen on Windows 10 laptop is not complicated. Here is a simple, step-by-step guide on how to take a print screen on a Windows 10 laptop. Firstly, press the ‘Windows Key + Print Screen’ keys on the laptop keyboard. This will immediately take a screenshot of the entire screen and the image will be saved to the Screenshots folder on your Windows 10 laptop. Secondly, if you want to take a screenshot of just a portion of the screen, press the ‘Windows key + Shift + S.’ This will bring up a crosshair cursor which you can use to select a specific area of the screen. Once you have selected the area you want to capture, an image of the selection will be copied to the clipboard. Thirdly, if you want to capture a single window or app, press the ‘Alt + Print Screen’ keys. This will take a screenshot of the active window and the image will be saved to the clipboard. Lastly, if you want to capture a free-form area, press the ‘Windows Key + Shift + S.’ The cursor will give you the ability to select any area of the screen to capture. All these steps make taking a print screen easier on Windows 10 laptop.
