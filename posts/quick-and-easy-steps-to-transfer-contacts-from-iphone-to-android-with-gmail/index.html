Gmail is an email service provider that allows users to create their own email account. With Gmail, users can easily transfer contacts from iPhone to Android. Here are the quick and easy steps to get it done.

First, you need to set up a Gmail account. Sign up with your personal information and make sure you include a secure password to protect your account. After that, go to the settings menu of your iPhone and select “Accounts & Passwords”. Select “Add Account” and then “Google”. Enter in your Gmail credentials and then approve the device.

Next, head over to your Google account on your Android. Log in and open up the contacts app. Then, select “Contacts” from the top list. You’ll then be able to select which accounts you would like to sync. Choose your Gmail account and sync it. This will bring over the contacts you have stored on your iPhone.

Once the contacts have been transferred, you will see a notification on your screen notifying you that the process has been successful. You can then manage the contacts from the Contacts app or directly from Gmail.

Syncing contacts from an iPhone to an Android device can be done using Gmail. To do this, you must first export the contacts from the iPhone and then import them into Gmail. To sync the contacts to your Android device, open the Settings app and select “Accounts”. Tap “Add Account” and select “Google”. Enter your Gmail address and password, then tap “Sign In”. Finally, tap “Sync” and select “Contacts”. Your contacts will now be synced to your Android device.
