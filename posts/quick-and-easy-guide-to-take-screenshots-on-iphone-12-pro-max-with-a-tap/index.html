I. Introduction#

Taking screenshots on your iPhone 12 Pro Max is faster and easier than ever before. With the new iOS 14 update, you can simply tap the back of your device to take a screenshot. This quick and easy guide will show you how to take screenshots with just a tap of your phone.

First, make sure you have the latest iOS version installed on your iPhone. To do this, go to Settings > General > Software Update. Once you’ve updated your phone, head to the Accessibility settings. Scroll down and tap on Back Tap to open the settings.

Next, select the action you want to use for taking screenshots. You can select from either Double Tap, Triple Tap, or Long Press. Once you’ve picked, you’re ready to take a screenshot. Simply tap the back of your iPhone twice (or however many times you chose), and your device will capture the screen.

Lastly, you can find the screenshot you just took in the Photos app. To access it, open Photos, tap the thumbnail in the lower-left corner, and then tap the screenshot to open it. From there, you can share, edit, or delete the screenshot as you please.

By following these steps, you can take screenshots quickly and easily on your iPhone 12 Pro Max. With a single tap of the back of your device, you can capture whatever is on your screen.
