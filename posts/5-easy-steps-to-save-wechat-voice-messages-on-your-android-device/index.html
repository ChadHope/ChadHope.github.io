Wouldn’t it be nice if there was an easy way to save voice messages from WeChat on our Android devices? Well, there is! With just a few simple steps, you can save WeChat voice messages on your Android device and have them at your fingertips whenever you want. Here are the five easy steps to do it.

First, open the WeChat app and log in to your account. Then, find the conversation you want to save the voice message from and click on it. You’ll see a list of all the voice messages sent and received in that conversation. Select the one you want to save and press the “More” button on the top right corner of the screen.

Second, a menu will pop up with several options. Select “Save to device” and the voice message will be saved to your phone’s local storage. You can access it anytime from your file manager.

Third, if you want to easily access the voice message from any device, go to the “More” menu and select “Export” instead. You can then choose to export the voice message to your cloud storage, such as Google Drive or Dropbox. This way you can safely store the voice message and access it from any device.

Fourth, if you want to share the voice message with someone else, you can copy the link generated by exporting the message and send it to them. This way, they can listen to your voice message without having to download it.

Finally, if you want to store the voice message on your computer, go back to the “More” menu and select “Send to Computer”. This will allow you to send the voice message to your computer over Wi-Fi. The voice message will be stored in the “WeChat” folder on your computer, where you can access it anytime.

That’s it! Just five easy steps to save WeChat voice messages on your Android device. Now you never have to worry about losing a conversation or forgetting what was said. With these simple steps, you can have all your conversations at your fingertips, whenever you need them. .
