1. Introduction to Taking Screenshots on Your Sony Android TV#

Taking screenshots on your Sony Android TV is one of the most underutilized features of the device. It can be used to quickly grab reference images, for photo sharing, or even to document what you are watching. The process is easy and straightforward, but there are a few tricks that can make it even easier. This ultimate guide outlines everything you need to know about taking screenshots on your Sony Android TV.

To start, you can use either the remote control or the Settings menu. To use the remote control, press and hold the “Back” and “Home” buttons simultaneously. This will take a full-screen screenshot of whatever is currently on the screen. If you are using the Settings menu, select “System Settings”, then “Screen Mirroring” followed by “Take Screenshot”. This will take the same full-screen screenshot as the remote control method.

A neat trick for taking screenshots of specific regions is to download the Android TV Remote Control app. This app allows you to take screenshots of any rectangle of your choosing, as well as easily mark up and share them with friends.

If you want to make sure you don’t lose your screenshots, you can save them to storage. Your screenshots will be stored in the “TV Home Menu” under “Screenshots”. You can also save them to a USB drive, which is a great way to save screenshots for later use.

These are the basics of taking screenshots on your Sony Android TV. Taking screenshots can be a great way to save and share your memories, and this step-by-step guide has everything you need to know to get started.
