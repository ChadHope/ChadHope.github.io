1. How to Set Up Your Google Account#

Google Account is an essential part of your Android and iOS devices. It is the gateway to the wealth of services Google offers for its users. Setting up your Google Account allows you to sync your contacts, emails and other content across your devices. In this tutorial, we will show you how to set up your Google Account for transferring contacts from Android to iPhone.

The first step is to open the Settings app on your Android device. Here you will see a list of option and one of them will be Accounts & Sync. Tap on this option to proceed. Now you will be asked to add a Google account. Tap the option and follow the instructions and fill in the required information. Once you have entered the credentials, you will be logged into your Google account.

Now that your Google Account is set up, you need to enable the option to sync Contacts. On the same Accounts & Sync window, tap on the option that says Sync Contacts and make sure it is enabled. Lastly, tap on the Sync Now option to start the process. This will start the syncing process for all your contacts.

Once the syncing is complete, you can now transfer the contacts from your Android to your iPhone. On your iPhone, open the Settings app and go to Accounts & Passwords. Here you will need to add your Google account. Fill in your credentials and tap the option to sync contacts. This will add all the contacts from your Android to your iPhone.

With this step-by-step guide, you can easily transfer contacts from Android to iPhone using Google. Just make sure the Google account is set up on both devices and you will be able to sync and transfer your contacts easily.
