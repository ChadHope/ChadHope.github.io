2. Tips to Take Great Screenshots on Windows Surface Pro 4#

Taking screenshots from your Windows Surface Pro 4 is a great way to capture what’s on your screen and save it for future use. Whether you’re capturing an image or video, here are a few tips to help you take the best screenshots possible.

First, use the Windows key and volume down key together to capture the entire screen. This will quickly capture the entire image, so you don’t have to worry about cropping or adjusting the size.

Next, use the Windows key and the PrtScn key to capture a rectangular region of your screen. This will allow you to select the exact area that needs to be captured with precision.

You can also use the Windows key and the Print Screen key together with the shift key to capture the active window. This will capture only the window that is currently active and ignore everything else.

Finally, use the Windows key and the PrtScn key together with the Alt key to capture the individual items on the screen. This will allow you to capture specific elements like text, icons, and other elements on the screen.
