Q1: How do I transfer photos from iPhone to Android via WiFi?

A1: You can transfer photos from iPhone to Android via WiFi by following these 5 simple steps:

  1. On your Android device, open the ‘settings’ menu and turn on ‘Wi-Fi’.
  2. On your iPhone, open the ‘Settings’ app, tap ‘Personal Hotspot’ and turn it on.
  3. On your Android device, connect to the iPhone’s hotspot in the Wi-Fi settings.
  4. Open the ‘Photos’ app on your iPhone and select the photos you want to send to your Android device.
  5. On the same Photos app, tap ‘Share’, then select your Android device from the list of available devices.

Q2: What do I need to transfer photos from iPhone to Android via WiFi?

A2: To transfer photos from iPhone to Android via WiFi, you will need:

  • An Android device with Wi-Fi connection
  • An iPhone with Personal Hotspot turned on
  • The Photos app installed on both devices

Q4: How do I turn on Personal Hotspot on my iPhone?

A4: To turn on Personal Hotspot on your iPhone, go to the ‘Settings’ app, tap ‘Personal Hotspot’ and toggle the switch to ‘On’.
