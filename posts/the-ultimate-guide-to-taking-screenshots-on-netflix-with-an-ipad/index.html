1. How to Take Screenshot on Netflix with an iPad#

Taking screenshots on Netflix from your iPad can be a great way to capture a memorable scene from the show or movie you are watching, or make a reference to share with others. To begin, open Netflix on your iPad and make sure you are on the show or movie you wish to screenshot. At the bottom of the screen, you should see a “More” button. Tap the “More” button and select “Screenshots” from the list. Once you tap, your iPad screen will briefly flash white, indicating that you have successfully taken a screenshot. The screenshot will appear in your Photos app. On the Photos app, swipe left and click the “Screenshots” folder to view your screenshot. Your screenshot is now ready to be shared with friends or saved on your iPad.
