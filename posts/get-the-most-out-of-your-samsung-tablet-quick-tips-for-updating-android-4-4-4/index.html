- How to check your current Android version#

If you own a Samsung tablet, chances are you already know the importance of keeping your device updated with the latest version of Android 4.4.4. It provides you with the latest features and security fixes, plus you’ll get the most out of your device. The process of manually checking your current Android version can be done in a few simple steps.

First, you will need to open your tablet’s settings. Once you are in the Settings menu, select the “ About Device” option. On the About Device page, look for an entry that reads either “ Software Version” or “ Android Version”. Here you will be able to view which version of Android you are currently running.

Next, you will need to visit the Google Play Store. The version of the Android operating system installed on your device must match that of the version supported by the apps and services provided by the Google Play Store. To find out which version is available as an update, tap the “My Apps & Games” option from the main page. Here you can check for updates to your current version of Android.

Finally, you can also use the Samsung Kies app to update your device to the latest version of Android 4.4.4. Kies is an official Samsung app that helps you to download updates and patches to your device. Once the app is open, it will automatically search for any available updates for your device. Be sure to connect your device to the internet before using Kies.

Finally, once the update is finished installing, you will need to restart your device for the changes to take effect.
