1. Introduction to Screenshots#

Taking screenshots is an essential part of any computer user’s work. Whether it’s for troubleshooting errors or to capture a funny moment, screenshots are a great way to quickly save information or even share it with friends or colleagues. In this article, we will discuss 5 easy steps to take screenshots on an Acer laptop. All you need to do is keep a few basic steps in mind and you will be able to capture screenshots in no time.

Firstly, open the window which you would like to take a screenshot of. Ensure that you have the appropriate and necessary information, e.g. text or images you would like to capture, displayed. Press the “PrtScr”, “Alt+PrtScr” or “Fn+PrtScr” depending on your laptop model. This will capture the window that is open on your screen.

Secondly, you can also capture a specific area of your screen by pressing the “Windows Key+Shift+S”. This will take a screenshot of any selected area on the screen. Once you select the area you would like to capture, it will be copied to your clipboard. Paste it onto a document or image editor and save the screenshot.

Thirdly, to capture the entire screen, press “Windows key+PrtScn”. The screen will appear to ‘freeze’, confirming that the screenshot has been taken. You can locate this screenshot in the “Pictures” folder in a folder titled “Screenshots”.

The fourth step is to capture the active window. Click on the window you would like to capture and press “Alt+PrtScr”. Once pressing this combination, the image of that window will be copied to your clipboard. Paste the image onto a document or image editor where you can save the screenshot.

The last step is to take a screenshot of the entire computer screen. Press the “Windows key+Ctrl+PrtScr”. This will save a screenshot to the “Screenshots” folder found in your “Pictures” folder. After following these 5 easy steps, you will be able to take screenshots of your Acer laptop with ease.
