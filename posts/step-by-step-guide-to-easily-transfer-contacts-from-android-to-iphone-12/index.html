2. Transfer Contacts from Android Phone to iPhone 12#

Being an Android user looking to switch to the new iPhone 12 can be daunting. One of the first questions you might have is how to transfer your contact list to your new iPhone. Luckily, this process has been made much easier with Apple’s Move to iOS app. The following step-by-step guide will help you easily transfer your contacts from your Android phone to your new iPhone 12.

First, make sure both of your devices are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. On your Android device, open the Move to iOS app and tap Continue. Agree to the terms and conditions. On your iOS device, tap Move Data from Android. A ten-digit code will appear on the Android device. Enter this code on your iOS device, and then tap Next.

Once the connection is established, select the data that you want to transfer. This includes Mail, Contacts, Calendar, Photos, Videos, and more. Once you’ve chosen the items, the transfer will begin.

When the transfer is complete, you’ll be asked to enter your Apple ID. Enter your Apple ID and password, and tap Next. Once this step is complete, the setup process is finished. You can now use your iPhone 12 with all of your contacts from your Android device.

By following these steps, you can easily transfer your contacts from your Android phone to your new iPhone 12. With just a few clicks, your contacts will be ready for use on your new device.
