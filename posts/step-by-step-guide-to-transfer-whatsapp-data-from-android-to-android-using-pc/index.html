1. Preparing the Necessities#

If you are looking for an easy way to transfer your WhatsApp data from Android to another Android device using PC, then this tutorial is for you. First, download and install the Android SDK platform-tools on your computer. Next, enable USB debugging on your Android device and connect it to your computer. Afterwards, open the platform-tools folder and execute the command “adb devices” to make sure your device is detected. Once that is done, type the command “adb backup -f .ab -all” to create a backup file of your WhatsApp data. Finally, transfer the backup file to your other Android device and execute the command “adb restore .ab” to restore the data. With these simple steps, you can easily transfer your WhatsApp data from Android to another Android device using PC.
