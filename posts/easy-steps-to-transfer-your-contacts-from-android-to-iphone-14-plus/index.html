First, connect your Android device to your computer with a USB cable. Once your phone is connected, you’ll need to back up your contacts to Google on your computer. To do this, open the “Settings” app on your Android phone and select “Accounts.” From there, you should be able to find your Google account and select it. Once you’ve done that, select “Sync Contacts” and select “Back up now.”

Once your contacts have been backed up on Google, you can then move them to your iPhone 14 Plus. To do this, open the “Settings” app on your iPhone and select “Contacts.” From there, choose “Import,” then select “Google.” Enter your Google account credentials, then choose “Sign in.” On the next screen, select “Agree” and your contacts will be imported to your iPhone.

And there you have it – your contacts have been successfully transferred from Android to your iPhone 14 Plus! 2. Steps to Transfer Contacts#

Switching from Android to iPhone 14 Plus can be a daunting task, given that contacts from one platform have to be transferred to another. This is especially true when it comes to contacts, as most people need their contacts list up and running quickly. Fortunately, the process of transferring contacts from Android to iPhone 14 Plus is relatively straightforward. Here’s how to do it in four easy steps.

Firstly, make sure both your Android and iPhone 14 Plus are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Now, open your Android’s Contacts app and select the Menu button in the top-right corner. From the drop-down menu, select Accounts and then your Google account. Now select More and then Export. Select all the contacts that you want to export from Android and hit OK.

Secondly, open the Settings on your iPhone 14 Plus and go to the Mail, Contacts, Calendars section. Select Add Account and choose Google from the list. Enter your Google account credentials and toggle on the option to sync contacts. The contacts will then be automatically synced with your iPhone 14 Plus.

Thirdly, open the Contacts app on your iPhone 14 Plus, select the “Groups” button in the top-left corner and toggle on the option to show All contacts. Now, select the “Import” button in the top-right corner and choose “From device storage”. You will then be asked to grant contacts permission, hit “Allow” and select the imported contacts file. Your contacts should now be successfully imported to your iPhone 14 Plus.

Finally, delete the contacts file from your iPhone 14 Plus by going to the Files app on your iPhone 14 Plus, hitting the “Edit” button and selecting the file. Now, hit the “Delete” button and choose “Move to Trash”. Your contacts should now be successfully moved from your Android to iPhone 14 Plus.
