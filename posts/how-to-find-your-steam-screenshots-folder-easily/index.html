1. Explaining Where to Find Your Steam Screenshots Folder#

Do you take a lot of screenshots with Steam? It can be difficult to navigate the different folders that Steam stores your screenshots in. But fret not, here’s how you can easily find your Steam screenshots folder.

First, open Steam. Then, click on Steam>Settings at the top left corner of the window. In the Settings window, select ‘In-Game’ from the top menu and then tick the ‘Enable Steam Overlay while in-game’ box. Once this is done, all screenshots you take while playing a game will be stored in the Steam screenshots folder.

The default Steam screenshots folder is usually located in your main Steam folder. This is usually something like C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\userdata[your Steam ID number]\760\remote. You may also find the screenshots folder in the main ‘Pictures’ or ‘Photos’ folder on your computer.

If you open this folder you’ll see all of the screenshots you’ve taken since you activated Steam’s screenshot function. You can use this folder to easily access and store your screenshots.
