1. Step by step Tutorial to Restore WhatsApp Backup from Android to Iphone with Google Drive#

Restoring WhatsApp backup from an Android device to an iPhone with Google Drive is a relatively simple process. First, open the Google Drive app on your Android device and log in to the same account that is linked to your iPhone. You can then head over to the ‘Backup’ tab and select the WhatsApp backup that you want to restore. Once you have made the selection, the backup will begin downloading to your Android device. After it has finished downloading, you will need to log into your Google Drive account on the iPhone and locate the backup. Finally, open the WhatsApp app on the iPhone and select Restore Backup from the settings menu. This should start the restoration process, and your WhatsApp messages from the Android device should now appear on the iPhone.
