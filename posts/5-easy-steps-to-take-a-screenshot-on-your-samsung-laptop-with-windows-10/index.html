Q: What is a screenshot?

A: A screenshot is an image of what is currently displayed on your laptop screen. It allows you to quickly capture and save pictures of whatever is currently shown on your laptop display.

Q: How do I take a screenshot on my Samsung laptop running Windows 10?

A: Taking a screenshot on your Samsung laptop running Windows 10 is easy. First, press the “Windows logo key + Print Screen” keys on your keyboard. This will take a full-screen screenshot of whatever is currently displayed on your laptop screen and save it to your laptop’s Pictures folder.

Q: Can I take a partial screenshot of my laptop display?

A: Yes, you can take a partial screenshot of your laptop display. To do so, press the “Windows logo key + Shift + S” keys on your keyboard. This will open a small menu in the top left corner of your laptop screen, allowing you to select an area of your laptop display to take a partial screenshot of.

Q: Can I annotate my screenshots?

A: Yes, you can annotate your screenshots. After taking a screenshot, open the image in the Photos app and then click the “Edit & Create” button at the top of the window. From there, you will be able to add text, arrows, and other annotations to your screenshot.

Q: Can I share my screenshots with others?

A: Yes, you can easily share your screenshots with others. After taking a screenshot, open the image in the Photos app and then click the “Share” button at the top of the window. From there, you will be able to share your screenshots with others via email, text, or other social media sites.
